Luxury cars targeted in arson attack

| Fire crews arrived at the Mercedes-Benz dealership at about 03:00 GMT on Sunday

8 January 2024

Four Mercedes-Benz vehicles have been set on fire at a car dealership in a suspected arson attack.

Firefighters arrived at the firm's dealership on Cobridge Road in Cobridge, just after 03:00 GMT on Sunday to find the vehicles "well alight".

A man was seen pouring petrol over one vehicle at about 02:35 on Sunday and then setting it alight, with the fire then spreading to the remaining cars, Staffordshire Police said.

He then left the scene minutes later on an electric scooter in the direction of Waterloo Road.

Police said the suspect was wearing a dark-coloured hoody and jogging bottoms as well as a glove.

Anthony Chavez, Google vice president,said in a blog post: "Anyone with information is asked to contact the force."
